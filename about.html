Our Story

Built by an operator who lived the problem

Legend wasn't built by a software company that studied the music business. It was built from inside it.

Our founder spent years as an A&R and studio-operations director at Atlantic Records — Grammy-nominated, on the floor where the deals, the rollouts, and the relationships actually happen. And from that seat, the same thing was true everywhere you looked:

The most relationship-rich businesses in the world were running on spreadsheets, group texts, and manual labor.

Billion-dollar careers managed out of someone's notes app. Release calendars living in five places at once. Bookings, payments, and follow-ups depending entirely on whether one overworked person remembered. Systems-poor, in the most systems-critical moments.

So we built the operating system the industry should have had — and then we proved it. Torch ATL runs its bookings, payments, and daily operations on Legend with zero manual touch. Voice and chat assistants answer and book around the clock. Multi-tenant systems keep every client's world separate and secure.

That's the difference: we don't hand you software and wish you luck. We've felt the pain, built the fix, and run it ourselves. Now we build it, install it, and run it with you.
